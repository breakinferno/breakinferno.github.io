题目

实现一个LazyMan,可以以以下方式调用：
LazyMan(‘LvFei’)
输出：Hi!That is LvFei!

LazyMan(‘LvFei’).sleep(10).eat(‘dinner’)
输出：Hi!That is LvFei!
//等待十秒
Wake up after 10s
Eat dinner~

LazyMan(‘LvFei’).eat(‘breakfast’).eat(‘dinner’)
输出：Hi!That is LvFei!
Eat breakfast~
Eat dinner~

LazyMan(‘LvFei’).sleepFirst(5).eat(‘dinner’)
输出：//等待5s
Hi!That is LvFei!
Eat dinner~

依次类推。
